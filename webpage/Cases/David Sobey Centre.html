Project overview

HSFM × The Shelf at Halifax Seaport Farmers’ Market

From cruise-season tourists to a younger, local-first community market.

Challenge

Revenue leaned on cruise tourists and older regulars. Weekdays were quiet and social reach was low.

My role

Co-led concept and growth strategy with the David Sobey Centre team.

Approach

  • Mapped who comes when – locals vs. cruise visitors.
  • Used a light Five-Forces view to place HSFM in the market.
  • Designed low-cost pilots for young locals (uni link, food trucks, chill area).

Impact

  • 3-step path to more revenue and younger footfall.
  • Shifted HSFM to an “incubator for local vendors”, not just a landlord.
  • Clear pilots HSFM can test next season.
