Project snapshot

Building momentum for a sustainability challenge

Supporting student teams to turn SDG ideas into competition-ready pitches.

Challenge

Many students knew the SDGs from class, but few translated them into concrete impact ideas or joined the Hult Prize competition.

My role

Led campus outreach and training as a Hult Prize Campus team member, connecting students, faculty and the global competition.

Approach

  • Organised info sessions and training on SDGs and impact business basics.
  • Hosted hands-on workshops on problem framing, pitch structure and storytelling.
  • Provided 1:1 feedback and mentoring to teams as they shaped their ideas.

Impact

  • Increased training session attendance by ~10% compared with regular university SDG seminars.
  • Supported more teams to move from “idea” to competition submission.
  • Deepened awareness of sustainability goals in the student community.
