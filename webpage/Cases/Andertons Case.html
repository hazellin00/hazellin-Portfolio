Project overview

Customer analytics for Andertons Music Co.

From raw transaction logs to a focused, data-backed growth plan.

Challenge

Andertons had rich transaction data but no clear view of customer segments, product affinities or churn risk. The brief: turn the data into simple, actionable recommendations for marketing and revenue.

My role

Led the analytics work: structured the notebook in Google Colab, explored the data, built segments and shaped the final story for non-technical stakeholders.

Approach

  • Cleaned and joined customer & transaction datasets.
  • Mapped high-value segments, product bundles and churn-risk groups.
  • Prioritised insights into a roadmap of experiments (cross-sell bundles, reactivation flows, loyalty rewards).

Impact

  • Achieved a 1.0 (A+) for the digital analytics & AI strategy proposal.
  • Linked complex data to clear commercial actions: who to target, with what offer, and through which channel.

Analytics deck & Colab notebook
