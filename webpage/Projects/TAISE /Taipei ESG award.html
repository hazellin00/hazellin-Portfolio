The 7th TGE Micro-Movie Festival — Opening Video & ESG Awards Report

The 7th TGE Festival Awards Ceremony was the first major project I was honored to be part of from beginning to end. From crafting the opening video for the ceremony to assisting with the final ESG Awards report, the whole experience made these months intense, challenging, and incredibly joyful — especially seeing companies gather around and light up when the opening video played.

Impact highlights:
  • Drove an 85% increase in corporate participation in ESG initiatives through effective reporting and strategic engagement with participating companies.
  • Boosted public awareness through creative outreach around the festival, leading to greater participation in climate change education and ESG storytelling.
  • Delivered a comprehensive ESG Awards report, analyzing companies’ sustainability goals and microfilm content to capture how ESG messages were communicated on screen.
This was the first end-to-end project I took part in at SIC and TAISE. The preparation period was intense — I had to juggle the opening video, compile the report, and handle constant last-minute issues, which was not easy. But thanks to smooth teamwork, a clear division of responsibilities, and the trust and support from my colleague Gift, the project lead, we were able to deliver a well-organized ceremony. Being part of this team is one of my favorite and most valuable memories from my internship.
