2023 Pacific Climate Change Forum — Welcome Banquet Deck

Planned and executed Taiwan’s first Climate Change Forum Welcome Banquet, designed as a flagship experience for international delegates, government representatives, and sustainability leaders. The deck and run-of-show document covered guest flow, staging, program timing, and cross-team coordination across protocol, media, and technical crews.

Impact highlights:
  • Delivered a high-impact welcome banquet that set a new benchmark for sustainability-driven community engagement.
  • Strengthened stakeholder relationships and reinforced TAISE’s role in leading climate change dialogue in the region.
In this role, I supported TAISE as an event coordinator and producer, aligning banquet logistics with the forum’s climate-focused mission. From vendor communication and staging details to on-site timing control, I helped strengthen collaboration between stakeholders and contributed to advancing green initiatives at both regional and global levels.
