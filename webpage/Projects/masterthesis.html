Research Overview

Wellness Waves: The Impact of Health Trends

My research explored how health claims and sustainability influence consumer behavior, applying the Stimulus‚ÄìOrganism‚ÄìResponse (SOR) framework alongside the AIDA marketing funnel. Based on data from 68 Taiwanese and 48 German respondents, I analyzed findings using Python to uncover deep behavioral insights.

Project Highlights (S.T.A.R.):
  • Situation: The rise of health & wellness trends is reshaping digital consumer behavior and purchasing decisions.
  • Task: Investigate how health-conscious consumers in Germany & Taiwan respond to labeling, sustainability claims, and CSR marketing.
  • Action: Designed a bilingual digital survey, developed an interactive e-commerce mockup, and analyzed engagement using the AIDA model.
  • Result: Uncovered key cultural differences in trust, purchase intent, and sustainability perception, providing actionable insights for global brands.
